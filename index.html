About Us

Medellin Pain Relief Clinic is the only ultimate destination for personalized Chiropractic care in the northern island of Cebu. Personalized treatment for those that suffer from chronic and acute health issues stemming from injuries, accidents, medical mistakes, chronic recurring illness and mysterious pain symptoms. Our advance techniques and technology along with decades of clinical experience help you achieve fast pain relief with long lasting results. Sick and suffering patients in pain of all ages are welcome.

Medellin Pain Relief is owned, operated and managed by Jennifer Richter. She has her Bachelors of Science in Nursing from the University of the Visayas here in Cebu. She along with her husband Dr. Ric have owned and operated a private Chiropractic clinic in America for 14 years prior to relocating to Cebu to retire 5 years ago.

Medellin Pain Relief clinic was established during the height of the world wide pandemic. Jen and Dr. Ric visited the Medellin mayor’s office to volunteer their services. After letting it be known that they were available for community service, Dr. Oliver Gimenez, Doctor of Health (DOH department) for Medellin area and head clinician at the Medellin’s RHU (Rural Health Unit) interviewed Jen about her services and success treating patients. Realizing the opportunity available for the community Dr. Gimenez immediately put Jen and Dr. Ric to work for the RHU. The first Rural Chiropractic Clinic for northern Cebu was born. Jen and Dr. Ric worked tirelessly on throngs of patients suffering in pain through the RHU on a voluntary basis for year or so. There was clearly an abundant need for this kind of treatment service for the community. Dr. Gimenez persuaded Jen and Dr. Ric to come out of retirement and with his blessing start a private Chiropractic clinic available for those sick and suffering in pain here in Medellin and beyond.

Medellin Pain Relief provides skilled Chiropractic treatment along with physical therapy with additional specialized treatment protocols that handle a variety of chronic/acute health issues. Dr. Ric is an American trained Doctor of Chiropractic who has been in private practice delivering treatment services to sick and suffering patients for over 45 years.

Medellin Pain Relief markets its services on our Facebook page and through FB paid video ads. We attract new patients throughout the Visayas territory. Currently Medellin Pain Relief’s video ads blanket the entire Cebu island areas. Our marketing design team creates a new video ad every month, posts either a new patient treatment or patient testimonials video daily to our page. You can also see our new short videos posted on Facebook and Instagram thrice daily.

If you or someone you know like a family member, friend or co-worker is sick and suffering in pain. Medellin Pain Relief would like the opportunity to help.

To make an appointment to consult with the doctor please contact Jen at 0917 169 7677 or click this website’s phone dial button on the first panel to reach Jen immediately.
